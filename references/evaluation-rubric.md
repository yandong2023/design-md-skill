# Evaluation rubric

Use this rubric when reviewing a page or README.

Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

## 1. Clarity
Can a new visitor quickly understand what the product is?

## 2. Audience fit
Is it clear who the page is for?

## 3. Value proposition
Does the page explain why the product matters?

## 4. Structure
Are sections ordered in a persuasive and useful way?

## 5. Proof
Does the page offer enough evidence to be credible?

## 6. CTA strength
Is the next action obvious and appropriate?

## 7. Developer trust
Does the page avoid hype and keep technical credibility?

## 8. Momentum
Does the page help the reader continue instead of stalling?

## Interpretation

- 4.5–5.0: strong
- 3.5–4.4: good with clear upgrade paths
- 2.5–3.4: usable but underperforming
- below 2.5: likely confusing or weak at conversion
